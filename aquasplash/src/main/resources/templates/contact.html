By Bus

Rishikesh city is about 230 kms away from Delhi and is well connected with buses. A bus journey may take about 5 - 7 hours depending on the traffic conditions. Taxis is a good option for a hassle free travelling

BY TRAIN

There are a number of trains from Delhi to Haridwar. Few trains are upto Rishikesh. However train journey is recommended from Delhi to Haridwar and from Haridwar a cab or bus can be taken for Rishikesh

BY AIR

There is an airport at Dehradun, The Jolly Grant Airport. One can travel by air from Delhi To Dehradun and from Jolly Grant Airport take a cab or a bus for Rishikesh. 50 mins air journey from Delhi to Doon
