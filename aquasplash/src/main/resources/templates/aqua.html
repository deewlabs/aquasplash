Welcome to Aquasplash Camp

Here at Aquasplash camp, we offer holiday packages par excellence at very promising rates. We are located near the Marine drive-rafting starting point in Rishikesh which makes it a lot easier to explore your surfing skills. We have professionally trained guides to enhance your white water rafting experience with us. Aquasplash is a one stop destination for all your adventure quests from Rafting ,Rappelling and Rock climbing to Kayaking and Hiking. We are surrounded by lush green teak forests and breathtaking views of the great Himalayan Mountains that provide an intimate connect with nature. We also offer Yoga and Meditation classes to relax yourself and rewind your routine.

Searching for the whereabouts already? Rishikesh, a city full of temples and centres for spiritual studies, now gets another identity with Aquasplash.

Camping

Enjoy your camp stay in luxury Swiss tents surrounded by mystical green forest at the foothill of Himalayas. Nothing beats the experience of gazing stars under clear sky with your friends, family and loved ones where the streams are gushing and birds are singing melodiously. Aquasplash camp offers the stay in Twin/Triple sharing basis.

Rafting

Rafting is one of popular adventure sports in Rishikesh which allures thousands of tourists from all over the world and Aquasplash Camp is the perfect place for adventure enthusiasts. We at Aquasplash camp offer various rafting packages to give you the thrilling opportunity to raft down the wild water rapids of River Ganga.

Lip Smacking Meals

Amidst the galvanizing environments of camp, our well trained chef along with kitchen staffs are committed to provide you scrumptious and nutritious delicacies to make your dining experience simply wonderful.All the meals are served hot and laid as buffet in central dining hall with beautiful ambience.

Yoga & Meditation

Yoga integrates the practices of postures, breathing techniques, lifestyle choices and mental exercises to bring the mind under control and enable living the life of peace and happiness. Our yoga facility is the heaven for those who seek a natural and peaceful environment to evolve in body, mind and spirit
