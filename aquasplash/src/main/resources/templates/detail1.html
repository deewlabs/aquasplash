Tour Itenary

Day 1

Reach Rishikesh which is also called as the "Gateway of Garhwal Himalayas" and also emerging "Adventure Capital of India" . Travel towards Badrinath on Rishikesh-Badrinath Highway for approximately 26 kms to reach the camp location which is also famously known as Marine Drive last point. It is suitable to reach camp by 1600Hrs in the camp . This day you will be welcomed by a hot cup of tea or coffee with snacks to make you comfortable in the camp. After a crisp briefing regarding the camp our team starts working on making your evening memorable. Our chef enjoys preparing lovely snacks and food while the others arranges bon fire and other indoor games. Sitting besides the bonfire under the blanket of stars with the shining moon will give you an amazing experience of being close to nature. You will have to carry your own drinks. Soft drinks and other beverages can be arranged on order on market price. After Bon fire Buffet dinner will be served . Undoubtedly you would never forget the experience of enjoying your meal sitting next to the bon fire for years to come coupled with unique experience of staying in comfortable swiss luxury tents catering all your comforts and need

Day 2

Beautiful morning with the chirping of birds . While you enjoy the lovely morning view of the lovely hills looking out of your tents we will serve you morning tea with snacks. You can take a stroll and enjoy the nature which is amazingly at its best . After a sound sleep we are sure you are geared up for the thrilling White Water Rafting . Buffet breakfast will be served to boost your energy level. After healthy breakfast and ready for the fun ahead we take you to the rafting commencing point where you will be provided all the safety gears and necessary briefing by the guide. On this stretch you will experience some amazing Rapids like RETURN TO SENDER , ROLLER COASTER , GOLF COURSE , BODY SURFING and so on. You will also get an opportunity of cliff jump enroute before the finish point. After rafting we take you back to camp where a delicious lunch is waiting for you. After lunch you are free to depart carrying endless memories and a promise of coming back again to our camps again
