Ways to Reach Us

By Bus

Rishikesh city is about 230 kms away from Delhi and is well connected with buses. A bus journey may take about 5 - 7 hours depending on the traffic conditions. Taxis is a good option for a hassle free travelling

BY TRAIN

There are a number of trains from Delhi to Haridwar. Few trains are upto Rishikesh. However train journey is recommended from Delhi to Haridwar and from Haridwar a cab or bus can be taken for Rishikesh

BY AIR

You can also travel by air from Delhi To Dehradun and from Jolly Grant Airport take a cab or a bus for Rishikesh. 50 mins air journey from Delhi to Doon. 1 hour journey from Doon airport to our camp

Tour Itinerary

Day 1

Reach Rishikesh and travel towards Badrinath on Rishikesh-Badrinath Highway for approximately 26 kms to reach the camp location which is also famously known as Marine Drive last point. It is ideal to reach camp by 1600Hrs. This day you will be welcomed with a hot cup of tea or coffee and snacks to make you comfortable in the camp. After a crisp briefing regarding the camp, our team starts working on making your evening memorable. Our chef enjoys preparing lovely snacks and food while the others arrange bon fire and other indoor games. Sitting beside the bonfire under the blanket of stars with the shining moon will give you an amazing experience of being close to nature. You will have to carry your own drinks. Soft drinks and other beverages can be arranged on order on market price. After Bon fire, Buffet dinner will be served in the camp. Undoubtedly you would never forget the experience of enjoying your meal sitting next to the bon fire for years to come coupled with unique experience of staying in comfortable Swiss luxury tents catering all your comforts and need.

Day 2

Beautiful morning with the chirping of birds . While you enjoy the lovely morning view of the lovely hills looking out of your tents we will serve you morning tea with snacks. You can take a stroll and enjoy the nature which is amazingly at its best . After a sound sleep we are sure you are geared up for the thrilling White Water Rafting . Buffet breakfast will be served to boost your energy level. After healthy breakfast and ready for the fun ahead we take you to the rafting commencing point where you will be provided all the safety gears and necessary briefing by the guide. On this stretch you will experience some amazing Rapids like RETURN TO SENDER , ROLLER COASTER , GOLF COURSE , BODY SURFING and so on. You will also get an opportunity of cliff jump enroute before the finish point. After rafting, we take you back to camp where delicious lunch is waiting for you. After lunch, we bid adieu, you with endless memories and us with hope of entertaining you again

If you haven't done a Rafting Expedition, you have done nothing in life

The most exciting and exhilarating way to run a river is to go for White Water Rafting Expedition. The mighty Himalayan Rivers gushes through range of the largest and most revered mountain range in the world over 3250 kilometers along India's northern frontiers right till the north eastern tip of India, finally meeting the ocean at Bay of Bengal. The abundance of Mountain Rivers makes India a world class white water adventure destination, with plenty of high grade rapids and exploratory possibilities.

Aqua Splash Camp is a leading professional White Water Rafting Expeditions outfit in the Indian Sub-Continent. For those high on adrenaline, appreciation for Mother Nature in heart and a disciplined soul (even if it is not, chances are that you will return much "enlightened"), only few experiences in life can match the introspective experience of a White Water Rafting Expedition

Imagine carrying your backpacks on your raft, paddling every day and camping out in the middle of nowhere, the good part is you are in safe hands! Team Aqua Splash Camp consists of some of the most experienced and well-trained River Rafting Guides and support staff. A support vehicle is always available there throughout the expedition..

Fun never ends over a River Rafting Expedition with Aqua Splash team. Every day you raft and paddle over the Grade 1 to 4+ rapids (depending on the river and sections), by late afternoon you reach a shore, pitch your dome/alpine tents, get involved in the chores at the camp (would you mind helping the camp cook chopping a few veggies, please?). Next morning, after a light but right breakfast you head again to the river, like a Cowboy all set for a fresh round of gallops, riding the mighty rivers, frothing rapids and getting mesmerized by the awe inspiring lush green surroundings, you will feel that this River Rafting expedition in the rivers in India is the best rafting experience you have ever had.

Tour Itinerary

Day 1 Haridwar to Sivpuri

Detrain at Haridwar if coming by train. Pickup From Haridwar Station and reach Aqua Splash Camp at Malla Kunthi near Marine Drive , rafting starting point. If travelling by road reach camp location by evening. Briefing regarding the expedition. Enjoy the bonfire with snacks and lip smacking dinner.

Day 2 Rishikesh to Devprayag

Leave from camp early morning at 0700 hrs by road to Devprayag after hearty Breakfast. Introduction with guides and safety kayakers after reaching Devprayag. A detailed safety briefing follows with instructions on paddling commands, negotiating the rapids and more.You are made to wear the safety gear. Rafting commences to Byasi beach. Halt for Lunch and prepare for night stay which involves pitching dome tents ,contribute for bonfire preperation, Snacks,drinks, Dinner and Overnight stay in Beasi Beach.

Day 3 Byasi to Kaudiala

After Breakfast, get involved with the team in getting the river rafts ready and packaging bags and closing dome tents. Commence rafting from 0930 hrs. Hit the mighty rapids of Ganges on your river rafting run of the day, get soaked in the winter sun and the mesmerizing Himalayan scenery. But, heyll Stay alert-negotiating the rapids is no child's play. You will reach Kaudiyala after paddling for about four hrs. Reach Kaudiala and lunch break at a lovely beach with mesmerising environs. Quick Lunch and proceed further to Marine Drive point. Negotiate the notoriously famous rapid-The Wall and Daniel’s Dip. Night halt at camp location. Bonfire, courage tales, and a well deserved meal follows. Refresh yourself for an eventful final day of rafting.

Day 4 Marine Drive to Rishikesh

Early morning breakfast to push some energy into your muscles. Get the raft rolling to negotiate the mighty rapids like Three Blind Mice, Crossfire, Body Surfing, Roller Coaster, Golf Course and Tee off to name a few. Gather some more adrenaline in your biceps, and let the grand finale be Cliff Jumping and Body Surfing before you reach the rafting end point.
