Welcome to Our Resort!

Aqua Splash camp is a luxury camp located at Mala Kunthi near Marine Drive - approximately 26kms from Rishikesh City on the Rishikesh-BadriNath Highway, surrounded by serene beauty of the lush green hills. Our Highly qualified and trained Rafting guides leave no stone unturned to provide you with the best of rafting adventure besides numerous other adventure activities while you have the most memorable and comfortable stay in twin/three persons sharing basis in the swiss luxury tents provided in our camp. We also take care of your tastes and provide you with the best of lip smacking meals which you shall remember for your life time. We have packages to suite your budget and time available for your stay at the camp. We alos cutomise your packages depending upon the days available for your vacation to suit your needs and budget
