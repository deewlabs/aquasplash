Camping & Activities

Whatever you love, whatever you want to experience, whatever you want to master â€“ you can do it all! Aqua Splash offers comprehensive water and land sports along with various camp activity programs, with state-of-the-art equipment. Have the best time with your friends and dare to experience the adventurous activities available at the camp regardless of whether you are a beginner or an expert, our skilled specialists ensure the highest levels of safety. Activities are available regularly throughout the day.

Rafting

Being relatively new sport in India, rafting has marked its spot on the adventure tourism map for decades now! There is nothing like the pure adrenaline rush one gets from riding the foaming white water rapids. At our camp, one can choose among three stretches of rafting between Marine Drive and Rishikesh. Some sections are with fast action white water rapids, others are calmer with opportunities for plenty of bodysurfing and bird watching. Fourth stretch of rafting can be covered during rafting expeditions

Kayaking

Another water sport that smacks of ingenuity & individuality is kayaking.This is a one sport requiring a big heart & great love for the water. At camp, we have our own kayaks with trained instructors to guide you while using the equipment and accompany you in case you would like to paddle around some placid stretches of the river around the camp. We also offer kayaking courses where one can learn the basic of kayaking to surf

Trekking

The camp is sourrounded by pristine forest with numerous trails and path that one can wander on foot. There are man villages scattered around within a 1-2 hours walking distance that can be visited and the natural surroundings offer great opportunity to spot some birds and wildlife. There are many caves as well as flowing streams that can be explored in the forest around . Hiking trips can be for a few hours, a full day or even 2-3 days with overnight stays organized in camps with all arrangements for meals,tents and sleeping bags

Cliff Jumping

We have many cliff jumping spots ranging from 5ft to 50ft all along the river side at the camp. Cliff jumping at the camp is with lifejacket to keep you sage and encourage even the non swimmers, and in the presence of a safety kayaker to bring you back to shore. As an activity it is an exhillerating experience as well as a challenge, leading to a great sense of achievement and overcoming fear

Rock Climbing

Rock Climbing is a sport in which participants climb up or across natural rock formation or artificial rock walls. Rock climbing is a physically and mentally demanding sport that tests strength, endurance, agility, and balance along mental control and determination

Rappelling

Rappelling is the controlled descent down a rope. Climbers use this technique when a cliff or slope is too steep or dangerous to descend without perfection. Done with the help of carabiners, harness, anchor points and safety ropes.

ZipLine

To add to your fun and adrenaline rush we have Zip line within our camp. We are the only camp in the area to have Zip line adventure facilty of 150 mtrs within our camp premesis. The thrill of doing Zip line adds to your unique experience while camping in our camp.

Archery

We have adequate archery equipment in the camp for both kids and adult to try your archery skills .instructors teach campers safety, various bow grips, shooting stances and accuracy. With both junior and senior-sizes compound bows, campers can participate in Bulls Eye challenges and group games.

Waterfalls

Unique to the camp, hike to the water fall is an activity that truly makes the stay at camp unforgettable. A walk upstream in knee deep flowing water to reach a short 6 feet high waterfall, climbing over it with the help of a rope fighting against the hard current and then wading through to reach a large waterfall is an experience that one will remember for a long time

Bonfire

Evening camp fire is a tradition in the camp and we have a dedicated camp fire area to gather in the evening . Complimentary snacks is served at the bonfire and we also offer veg and non-veg barbeque on order.

Fun Games

To cater for activity while you are in the camp we have catered for volleyball, Badminton, Carrom board, Dart Games, Chess, Frisbie and obstacle course like Burma Bridge, Sky Walk etc

Shooting

We have a dedicated firing range to shoot on targets with Air Rifles and Air Pistols to check you shooting skills

Rishikesh serves as the gateway to the Garhwal Himalayas. Enjoying a camping trip amidst the nature-rich environs of Rishikesh happens to be an extremely rejuvenating experience. During your camping adventure, you get to spend time in the midst of pine and deodar forests, gurgling brooks, sprawling meadows and fresh air. Given below are some dos and don'ts that need to keep in mind while enjoying your camping adventure in Rishikesh.

DO's Of Camping

  • Do make sure you get back to the campsite before dark
  • Do go hiking accompanied by at least one person. Make your co-travellers informed
  • Do remember to carry a geographical map of the region you are camping in
  • Do bring with you insect repellents
  • Do dress yourself in layers so that you may take off or take on to adjust with the temperature
  • Do carry with you sunscreen, good flashlight, additional batteries, extra bags etc..
  • Do try to leave the least carbon footprints
  • Do plan your food menu in advance, so that there is no hassle about what to cater specially when out in wilderness
  • Do pack out things like matches, toilet paper and cigarette butts before you leave the place
  • Do use a checklist so that you do not miss out on some essentials you will regret later

DONT's Of Camping

  • Don't enter the river without life jacket.
  • Don't ignore the directions given by the guide
  • Don't jump into the river from the raft unless guides allows you
  • Don't try to feed or harass the wildlife
  • Don't wear loose clothes while rafting
  • Don't try to enter a private property
  • No Alcohol before rafting
  • Try not to wear cotton clothes.They take time to dry
  • Don't throw litter in the camp area or on the beaches. Carry proper disposable bags for empty packets and cans
  • Don't forget to say thanks and buy a drink to the person who pulls you up the raft in case you flip
  • Don't wear any jewellery or costly item while on the raft
  • Don't remove life jacket through out the rafitng journey
  • Do not panic in case you slip from the raft .Our guides will pull you back
  • Do not forget to refer us
