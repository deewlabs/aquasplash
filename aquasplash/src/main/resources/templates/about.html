Rishikesh-Gateway Of Himalayas

Aquasplash Rafting camp is a luxury camp located at Mala Kunthi near Marine drive point (starting point of rafting) which is approximately 26 kms from Rishikesh on the Rishikesh - Badrinath highway, surrounded by mesmerizing beauty of the lush green hills. Our Highly qualified and trained Rafting guides leave no stone unturned to provide you with the best of rafting adventure beside numerous other adventure activities while you have the most memorable and comfortable stay in twin/ triple sharing basis in the swiss luxury tents provided in our camp. We also take care of your taste buds and provide you with the best of lip smacking meals which you shall remember for your life time. We have different packages to suit your budget and time available for your stay at the camp. We can customise the packages to meet your need and depending upon the number of days planned by you for your vacation.

Facilities in our camp

  • 12 luxury Swiss Tents which can accommodate 3 to 4 persons, each provided with light and charging point, water camper, Thermos, and with clean attached western toilets
  • Hot water is provided for bathing purpose
  • Clean Common toilets spread at two places in camp for easy access
  • RO water used for drinking and cooking purposes
  • Cameras fitted in camp for safety and security purpose
  • Dome tents available for those who would like to experience staying in them
  • Refrigerator available in case you need to preserve some eatables of yours
  • First Aid kit box alongwith stretcher available( we are sure you wont though require it)
  • Fire fighting equipment in place for safety
  • Zip- line of 150 mtrs within the camp
  • Obstacle course to negotiate
  • Firing Range to try your shooting skills with Air Rifle and pistol
  • Archery equipment for testing your skills
  • Camp games like volleyball, Carrom board, Dart games, badminton and so on
  • Stream running 50 mtrs from camp
  • Treks available for visit to local villages and forest
  • DJ Music for dance and fun
  • All best quality equipment and gear for rafting purpose
  • Dedicated vehicle Mahindra Camper to cater for any emergent need
